# MaximumCreativity Workflow

No constraints. Wild ideas welcome. Push boundaries.

## Steps

1. **Forget constraints** — Explicitly list and then ignore all limitations
2. **Wild ideas** — Generate 10+ ideas, the more absurd the better
3. **Pattern hunt** — What themes emerge from the wild ideas?
4. **Bridge back** — Which wild ideas contain kernels applicable to reality?
5. **Prototype concept** — Describe one wild-but-feasible concept in detail

## Output Format

```
# 🚀 Maximum Creativity: [Topic]

## Constraints We're Ignoring
- [constraint 1]
- [constraint 2]

## Wild Ideas (unfiltered)
1. [wild idea]
2. [wilder idea]
...

## Emerging Patterns
- [theme 1]
- [theme 2]

## Bridging to Reality
[Which kernels from wild ideas are actually usable]

## The Concept
**Name:** [catchy name]
**Core Insight:** [the idea]
**How It Works:** [description]
**Why It's Wild:** [what makes it novel]
**Why It Might Work:** [why it's not crazy]
```
