# Creative Thinking Principles

## 1. Diverge Before Converging
Generate many ideas before evaluating. Premature criticism kills creativity.

## 2. Build on "Yes, And..."
Instead of "No, but..." — take every idea and extend it. Bad ideas often lead to good ones.

## 3. Cross-Pollinate
Borrow from unrelated domains. How would a chef solve this? A musician? A biologist?

## 4. Invert the Problem
Instead of "How do we X?", ask "How do we make X impossible?" Then reverse it.

## 5. Constraint as Catalyst
Add artificial constraints to force creativity. "What if we had no budget?" "What if it had to fit in a tweet?"

## 6. Verbalized Sampling
Think out loud through multiple approaches. Name them, compare them, combine them.

## 7. Embrace Absurdity
The most creative solutions often start as "that's ridiculous." Push past the ridiculous to find insights.
