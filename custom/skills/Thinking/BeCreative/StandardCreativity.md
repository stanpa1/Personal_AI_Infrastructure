# StandardCreativity Workflow

Balanced creative exploration with structured output.

## Steps

1. **Reframe** — Restate the problem 3 different ways
2. **Diverge** — Generate 5-7 ideas using different techniques (SCAMPER, analogy, inversion)
3. **Cross-pollinate** — Pick 2 ideas and combine them
4. **Evaluate** — Rate ideas on novelty, feasibility, and impact
5. **Refine** — Develop top 2 ideas further

## Output Format

```
# 💡 Creative Exploration: [Topic]

## Reframes
1. [reframe 1]
2. [reframe 2]
3. [reframe 3]

## Ideas
1. **[Name]** — [description] (via [technique])
2. **[Name]** — [description] (via [technique])
...

## Combinations
- [Idea A] + [Idea B] = [New Idea]

## Evaluation
| Idea | Novelty | Feasibility | Impact | Score |
|------|---------|-------------|--------|-------|
| ... | ⭐⭐⭐ | ⭐⭐ | ⭐⭐⭐ | 8/9 |

## Top Picks (Refined)
### 1. [Best Idea]
[Detailed development]

### 2. [Runner Up]
[Detailed development]
```
