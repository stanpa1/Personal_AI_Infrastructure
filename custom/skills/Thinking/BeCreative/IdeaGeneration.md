# IdeaGeneration Workflow

Pure quantity brainstorm. No evaluation during generation.

## Rules
- No criticism during generation
- Build on others' ideas
- Quantity over quality
- Wild ideas welcome
- Aim for 15+ ideas

## Steps

1. **Set topic** — One clear sentence
2. **Speed round** — 15+ ideas in rapid fire, no filtering
3. **Cluster** — Group similar ideas
4. **Star** — Mark the 3 most promising
5. **Next step** — Pick one to develop further

## Output Format

```
# 🧠 Brainstorm: [Topic]

## All Ideas
1. [idea]
2. [idea]
... (15+ items)

## Clusters
**[Theme A]:** ideas #1, #4, #7
**[Theme B]:** ideas #2, #5, #9
...

## ⭐ Top 3
1. **[idea]** — [why it's promising]
2. **[idea]** — [why it's promising]
3. **[idea]** — [why it's promising]

## Next: Develop [chosen idea]
[Brief development notes]
```
