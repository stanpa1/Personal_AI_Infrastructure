# Quick Workflow

Single-round rapid assessment from all 4 perspectives.

## Trigger
User says "quick council", "quick perspectives", or time is limited

## Steps

1. **Frame** — restate problem in one sentence
2. **Table** — each agent gives position, key argument, and risk in compact format
3. **Recommendation** — 2-3 sentence synthesis

## Output
Use compact format from OutputFormat.md
